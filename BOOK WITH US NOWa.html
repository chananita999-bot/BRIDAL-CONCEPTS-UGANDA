Types of Events in Uganda

Explore the most important event categories celebrated across Uganda, with examples of ceremonies, festivals, and cultural gatherings.

We decorate all types of events, from traditional ceremonies to corporate functions, festivals, religious gatherings, sports events, and lifestyle celebrations.

🇺🇬 1. Cultural & Traditional Events

These celebrate Uganda’s many ethnic groups and kingdoms.

  • Imbalu Circumcision Ceremony – initiation ritual among the Bagisu
  • Empango Ceremony – marks the Omukama’s reign
  • Ekyooto Ha Mpango – revival of Tooro traditions
  • Buganda Royal Events – e.g., Kabaka’s birthday run

What we do:

  • Traditional décor styling with cultural fabrics and royal accents
  • Stage backdrops, ceremonial seating, and heritage table settings
  • Traditional floral arrangements and lighting for cultural stages

🎶 2. Music & Arts Festivals

Uganda has a growing creative scene.

  • Nyege Nyege Festival – internationally known music festival
  • Bayimba International Festival – music, dance, theater
  • Blankets and Wine Uganda – lifestyle + live music
  • Kampala Art Biennale – visual arts showcase

What we do:

  • Festival entrances, stage décor, and branded lounge areas
  • Artist green room styling, photo walls, and lighting effects
  • Art display spaces, chill-out zones, and social media-ready décor

🙏 3. Religious Events

Uganda is deeply religious, with major Christian and Muslim gatherings.

  • Uganda Martyrs Day – huge pilgrimage to Namugongo
  • Eid al-Fitr – marks end of Ramadan
  • Eid al-Adha
  • Christmas and Easter celebrations
  • Baptism and christening ceremonies – church blessings and family reception décor
  • Kuhinira – traditional welcome-home celebration for newborns

What we do:

  • Respectful church and mosque décor with elegant draping
  • Altar/ stage styling, seating plans, and ceremonial lighting
  • Seasonal accents and floral arrangements for worship gatherings
  • Baptism and baby naming décor with soft florals, candles, and dedication tables
  • Kuhinira styling with traditional fabrics, ceremonial tables, and welcome-home displays

🏛️ 4. National & Political Events

Important for patriotism and governance.

  • Independence Day Uganda
  • Liberation Day Uganda
  • Uganda General Elections
  • State of the Nation Address Uganda

What we do:

  • Patriotic stage décor, flags, and ceremonial furniture
  • VIP seating, podium styling, and official event signage
  • Lighting and audio-friendly décor for large public gatherings

🏃 5. Sports Events

From marathons to international competitions.

  • Kampala Marathon
  • Uganda Cup
  • CECAFA Cup
  • Local football matches (e.g., Uganda Cranes games)

What we do:

  • Entrance arches, finish line décor, and fan zones
  • Team branding, VIP lounges, and sponsor displays
  • Sports-themed lighting and stage settings for ceremonies

💼 6. Business & Trade Events

Focused on networking, innovation, and commerce.

  • Uganda International Trade Fair
  • Harvest Money Expo
  • ICT Innovation Week Uganda

What we do:

  • Exhibition booths, branded displays, and corporate lounges
  • Stage décor for presentations, panel discussions, and product demos
  • Networking spaces, lighting, and signage for polished business events

🎓 7. Educational & Academic Events

Held by schools, universities, and institutions.

  • Graduation ceremonies (e.g., Makerere University graduations)
  • Academic conferences and research symposiums
  • Career fairs and student exhibitions

What we do:

  • Stage décor, lecterns, and backdrop design for ceremonies
  • Exhibition spaces, branded visuals, and presentation zones
  • Graduate photo walls, seating layouts, and ceremonial lighting

🎉 8. Social & Lifestyle Events

Modern urban lifestyle events.

  • Fashion shows (e.g., Abryanz Style and Fashion Awards)
  • Comedy shows (e.g., Comedy Store Uganda)
  • Nightlife events, parties, and themed hangouts

What we do:

  • Runway décor, stage styling, and lounge areas
  • Theme-based lighting, bar styling, and VIP seating
  • Social media-ready backdrops, signage, and event props

🌍 9. Tourism & Wildlife Events

Promote Uganda as a travel destination.

  • Pearl of Africa Tourism Expo
  • Gorilla naming or conservation awareness events
  • National park festivals and travel expos

What we do:

  • Destination-themed décor, expo booths, and immersive displays
  • Photo zones, cultural motifs, and wildlife-inspired design
  • Lighting and signage that highlight tourism attractions

🎬 10. Film & Media Events

Growing film industry in Uganda.

  • Uganda Film Festival
  • Amakula International Film Festival

What we do:

  • Red carpet décor, screening lounges, and sponsor branding
  • Stage styling for awards, panels, and press conferences
  • Media-friendly backdrops, lighting, and hospitality spaces

In Uganda, events generally fall into these 10 major categories: Cultural & traditional, Music & arts, Religious, National & political, Sports, Business & trade, Educational, Social & lifestyle, Tourism, Film & media.
